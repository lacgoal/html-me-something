Run, run, run, as fast as you can...

I enjoy running.

Not so much on the roads or on pavement, but on dirt, on a rocky path...

On the trails, I feel like a kid running amok. The sights, smells, sounds...

Give me a hill and I'll meet you at the top.

A flat stretch and I'll meet you at the end.

I may not look like your "typical runner" capable of fast speed.

I'm fortunate to carry extra "fuel" for the long haul, going the distance.

She's going for distance...

Each week, the miles add up, and I get one step closer to my goal of running a trail marathon. My goa is to run the marathon around April/May in celebration of my craziness and of my birthday.

The Kansas City area has an awesome group, called the Trail Nerdz, who have a long list of events planned for throughout the year. The distances range from the "beginner" 5 k to the "veteran" 100 miler. My upcomign races include a 4-miler, a 10k, and then a 10-miler at the beginning of Dec.

My plans don't end at running the marathon. Oh no, I'm crazy enough to think about running a 50k, then a 50 miler, then a 100 k, then 100 miles... It's hard to explain why, but how I feel after each trip out on to the trails... That let's me know I'm doing something good and I have no plans of stopping. Maybe a good amount of walking, but not stopping.
