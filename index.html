"You have to put up with the shit, to smell the flowers."

-Me

Two years ago, I was back home, in upstate New York, and I wasn't happy with the path I was on. I was surrounded by negative people and stuck in a job that wasn't a good fit for me. Mentally, I felt drained and physically, I felt gross. I had lost sight of my goals and ideas. Something needed to change.

I began to list out the things that I enjoyed, goals that had been put on the backburner, and ideas of where I saw myself. This led to me outlining a plan. A plan of where I saw myself in 5 years.

    5-Year Plan
  1. Career
  2. Happy
  3. Athletic
  4. New
  5. G
  6. Education

Change was needed in order for this plan to take place. I couldn't stay in the same place otherwise the chance of falling back in to the same rut would be too easy. I dropped everything and moved cross-country, to the midwest, to begin a new path.
